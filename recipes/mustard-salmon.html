Mustard Salmon

Description

Easy Mustard Salmon made Gluten Free.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lbs skin-on salmon fillets
  • 2 Tbs olive oil
  • 2 Tbs freshly squeezed orange juice
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 2 tsp Gluten Free Dijon mustard
  • 1/4 tsp salt

Steps

  1. To make the mustard sauce, whisk 2 Tbs olive oil with the orange juice, mustard, garlic and 1/4 teaspoon salt. Set aside.
  2. Pat the salmon dry and brush with olive oil before seasoning both sides with salt & pepper.
  3. Heat a cast-iron skillet over medium-high heat until it's very hot.
  4. Cook salmon skin-side down over medium-high heat about 3-5 mins. Using tongs flip the fillets and lower heat to medium and cook another 4-5 mins.
  5. Transfer to a plate and brush the hot fish with mustard sauce.
  6. Enjoy!
